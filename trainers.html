Coach Ryan Blake - Strength & Conditioning Specialist

Ryan brings 9 years of experience in strength and conditioning, helping clients build functional strength and achieve their fitness goals. As a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS), he focuses on proper form, progressive overload, and sustainable training methods.

Specialties: Strength Training, Powerlifting, Functional Fitness, Body Transformation

Certifications: CSCS, NASM-CPT, FMS Level 1

Coach Aisha Tan - Zumba & Group Fitness Instructor

Aisha brings infectious energy and 5 years of experience to her Zumba and cardio classes. Her background in dance and fitness allows her to create fun, engaging workouts that feel more like parties than exercise. She specializes in making fitness accessible and enjoyable for all levels.

Specialties: Zumba, Dance Fitness, HIIT Cardio, Group Training

Certifications: Zumba Instructor, ACE Group Fitness, CPR/AED

Coach Maya Patel - Yoga & Mobility Specialist

With 7 years of teaching experience and RYT-500 certification, Maya specializes in creating personalized yoga flows that improve flexibility, mobility, and mental clarity. Her approach combines traditional yoga principles with modern mobility techniques to help students achieve balance in both body and mind.

Specialties: Vinyasa Flow, Restorative Yoga, Mobility Training, Mindfulness

Certifications: RYT-500, Yoga Medicine, Functional Range Conditioning
